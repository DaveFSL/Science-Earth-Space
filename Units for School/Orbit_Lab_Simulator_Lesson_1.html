Detective's note: most people predict a spiral. The straight line surprises almost everyone — and it's exactly what Isaac Newton worked out: objects travel in straight lines unless a force pulls them off course. The orbit only exists because gravity keeps bending Earth's straight-line motion into a circle.

3 The Goldilocks problem
